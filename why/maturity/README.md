# Maturity

The POSITIVE DevRel Maturity Framework describes the operational maturity of a technical ecosystem.

The framework helps teams:

- understand the current state of an ecosystem
- identify operational constraints
- measure distributed capability
- assess ecosystem resilience
- understand dependency patterns
- and guide ecosystems toward self-organization

The maturity model is not simply about community participation.

It is about the development of shared operational understanding, collaborative capability, trust, autonomy, and collective resilience.

## Levels

### 1: Isolated

No shared operational understanding.

Knowledge is fragmented.
Dependency on centralized expertise is high.

### 2: Dependent

Operational understanding exists but remains centralized.

Practitioners rely heavily on:

- maintainers
- experts
- vendors
- centralized support

### 3: Contributing

Localized capability begins to emerge.

Practitioners:

- share solutions
- contribute examples
- improve systems
- document practices

### 4: Participating

Distributed operational knowledge exchange exists.

The ecosystem collaborates, teaches itself, and develops shared practices.

### 5: Self-organizing

The ecosystem becomes operationally autonomous.

Knowledge propagates organically.
Capability scales horizontally.
Practitioners coordinate independently.

## Pillars

Each STACK pillar has its own maturity dimension — a way of reading how well the ecosystem is developing along that axis. The tables in each linked document show what each level looks like in practice.

**Stewardship** — how well DevRel can see and tend the graph

* [Observations](maturity-observations.md) — ecosystem visibility and health monitoring
* [People](maturity-people.md) — knowing who is in the graph and connecting them

**Tutoring** — how effectively individual nodes are being strengthened

* [Skill](maturity-skill.md) — learning progression from personal experimentation to shared curriculum

**Autonomy** — how self-sufficient the ecosystem has become

* [Solutions](maturity-solutions.md) — the ecosystem's ability to solve its own problems without DevRel in the room

**Capability** — the collective load-bearing capacity of the graph

* [Voice](maturity-voice.md) — practitioners' ability to influence the technology's direction
* [Shared Enterprise](maturity-enterprise.md) — collective contribution to building the technology itself

**Kinship** — the quality and density of the edges

* [Innovation](maturity-innovation.md) — practitioners creating and sharing knowledge together
* [Inspiration](maturity-inspiration.md) — the ecosystem telling its own story publicly
