# Maturity

The maturity model describes the operational maturity of a technical ecosystem — not its community engagement, not its event attendance, not its follower count.

It measures something more fundamental: the degree to which the ecosystem can operate, teach itself, and solve its own problems without depending on DevRel or the vendor to route every question and connection.

The progression is one of graph topology. As an ecosystem matures, it moves from isolated nodes to a resilient, self-organising mesh.

## Levels

### 1: Isolated

No shared operational understanding. Knowledge is fragmented. Dependency on centralised expertise is high. Practitioners cannot find each other; every question routes back to the source.

### 2: Dependent

Operational understanding exists but remains centralised. Practitioners rely heavily on maintainers, experts, vendors, and centralised support. The graph has a hub — but everything routes through it.

### 3: Contributing

Localised capability begins to emerge. Practitioners share solutions, contribute examples, improve systems, and document practices. The first direct node-to-node edges appear.

### 4: Participating

Distributed operational knowledge exchange exists. The ecosystem collaborates, teaches itself, and develops shared practices. Guardians are visible and active.

### 5: Self-organising

The ecosystem becomes operationally autonomous. Knowledge propagates organically. Capability scales horizontally. Practitioners coordinate independently. DevRel's role shifts from tending to observing.

Each STACK pillar has its own maturity dimension — a way of reading how well the ecosystem is developing along that axis. The tables in each linked document show what each level looks like in practice.

**Stewardship** — how well DevRel can see and tend the graph

- [Observations](maturity-observations.md) — ecosystem visibility and health monitoring

- [Skill](maturity-skill.md) — learning progression from personal experimentation to shared curriculum

**Autonomy** — how self-sufficient the ecosystem has become

- [Solutions](maturity-solutions.md) — the ecosystem's ability to solve its own problems without DevRel in the room

- [Voice](maturity-voice.md) — practitioners' ability to influence the technology's direction
- [Shared Enterprise](maturity-enterprise.md) — collective contribution to building the technology itself

**Kinship** — the quality and density of the edges

- [Innovation](maturity-innovation.md) — practitioners creating and sharing knowledge together
- [Inspiration](maturity-inspiration.md) — the ecosystem telling its own story publicly
