# The Knight

The Knight is a trusted technical evaluator — an enterprise architect, senior engineer, or technology strategist whose job is to assess whether a technology is the right fit for their organisation. They have a commercial sensibility alongside their technical depth. They are listened to when technology decisions are being made.

Knights are not just evaluating the technology. They are evaluating whether it fits: into the architecture, into the compliance framework, into the organisational risk appetite, into the existing tooling ecosystem. Their judgement is about the whole picture, not just the performance benchmarks.

## What drives them

The Knight wants to make the right call and to be seen to make the right call. Their credibility inside their organisation rests on the quality of their technical judgements. They are thorough because thoroughness is what their role demands — and because a bad recommendation has consequences they will be accountable for.

They have influence that stretches beyond their own organisation. They speak confidently with counterparts facing similar problems. Their endorsement carries weight.

## Where they get stuck

The Knight's questions are not about features. They are about fit, risk, and architecture:

- What is this, and what problems is it designed to solve?
- How does it compare with what I already know — or don't know?
- Who else is using it, and at what scale?
- Will it interoperate with the rest of my landscape?
- What is the operational risk and overhead?
- How do I make the internal case for it?
- When do I need compliance approval?

Content that does not answer these questions at the depth the Knight needs will not reach them.

## What moves them forward

- Rationale and architectural patterns, not just feature lists
- Use cases at the scale and complexity they operate at
- Independent benchmarks and third-party assessments
- Testimonials and endorsements from peers they respect
- Trade press coverage that confirms the technology's position in the landscape

## In the ecosystem

The Knight occupies an interesting position — not yet a [Guardian](guardian.md), but a high-influence [Beneficiary](beneficiary.md) whose endorsement opens doors. When a Knight adopts something and speaks well of it, others follow. DevRel's role is to ensure the Knight has the depth of information they need to make a confident call, and to create pathways for Knights who become advocates to develop into full Guardians.
