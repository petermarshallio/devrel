# Connected Correspondent

**Aristotelian grounding:** Ethos — the instrument of credibility and trust.

**Real-world exemplar:** Louis Theroux. Has the remarkable ability to make people forget they are being heard. Genuinely curious, genuinely non-judgemental, genuinely interested — and people open up to him in ways they do not open up to others.

**Distinguishing characteristic:** Truth flows inward. The CC's gift is the non-transactional relationship — built without agenda, without quota, without a message to land — that creates the conditions for honest intelligence to surface. This draws on Polanyi's concept of tacit knowledge: "We know more than we can tell." Practitioners hold knowledge they cannot easily articulate. The CC creates the conditions in which that knowledge becomes visible.
