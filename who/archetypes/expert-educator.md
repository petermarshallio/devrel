# Expert Educator

**Aristotelian grounding:** Logos — the instrument of reason, knowledge, and evidence.

**Real-world exemplar:** David Attenborough. Takes something genuinely complex and makes it graspable — without dumbing it down, without losing the wonder, without pretending it is simpler than it is. The knowledge is real. The communication is a craft.

**Distinguishing characteristic:** Knowledge flows outward. The EE's gift is the ability to meet a practitioner exactly where they are and move them somewhere more capable — reducing cognitive load, scaffolding understanding, building confidence alongside capability. An EE can fill a room or fill a documentation site; the mode of expression varies, the instrument does not.
