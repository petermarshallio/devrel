# Welcome to the network

Everyone starts on the outside of this special group of friends.

For that, DevRel is full of [inspiration(programs/1-inspiration.md).

Walking the halls of How To Be At Our Party, you might be thinking, am I just gonna cruise right now, or am I thinking of actually handing over this bottle.

For that, DevRel is full of [information](programs/2-information.md). Information about how to eat the cake and information on what cakes people would love you to bring next time.

- **A Beneficiary** receives from the community — capability, confidence, solutions to problems. You _take_ without expecting to pay _any_ price.
- **A Donor** contributes to the community — code, knowledge, feedback, advocacy, time. You _give_ without expecting _anything_ back.

You might expect every member of the network to start off as a beneficiary. And most remain beneficiaries, too. That's the (80/20) way of life.

Founders? They're the first donor. They're special. They are the people who answer questions, write the articles, speak at the meetups, contribute fixes. And _every_ beneficiary who starts to donate is to be celebrated. But for a _very particular reason_: they also _bring us together_.

For them, DevRel is full of opportunities for [involvement](programs/3-involvement.md) and [innovation](programs/4-innovation.md).

And finally there's a special box to put people in. The **guardian**.

They embody that moment when the network in which DevRel set sail every day is finally bigger than the sum of its parts.

For them, this isn't about the individual any more - it's about the whole damn organism. Once this comes online in their brainboxes, they become _structurally load-bearing_. They truly want to make the network resilient in a way that purely beneficiary participation never can. When a Guardian moves organisations, they carry their relationships with them because they matter to them _personally_. When they speak, others listen. When they write code that doesn't work, there's no outcry: there is a rush to action.

These are the champions, the heroes.

And somebody in DevRel _must be_ one of these people.
